Pull Day

Pull days focus on exercises that target the back and biceps. You'll perform pulling movements that improve posture, strength, and build an impressive back and arms.

Sample Pull Day Exercises

  • Lat Pulldown (Grip Slightly Wider than Shoulder Width) – Focuses on the lats; think about pulling with your elbows, not your biceps.
  • Seated Cable Row (Elbows Flared Out) – Targets the upper back and rear delts; keep your chest tall.
  • Cable Rope Face Pulls or Seated Rear Delt Machine – Strengthens the rear delts and improves posture and shoulder health.
  • Incline Bench Bicep Curls or Barbell Drag Curls – Focuses on the long head of the bicep for better peak development.
  • Preacher Curls (Machine, Barbell, or Dumbbell) – Isolates the biceps and eliminates momentum for strict form.
  • Hammer Curls – Targets the brachialis (deep arm muscle) and brachioradialis (forearm) for overall arm thickness.
