Leg Day

Leg days focus on building the strength and endurance of your lower body muscles including the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves.

Sample Leg Day Exercises

  • Barbell Squats – Targets the quads, glutes, hamstrings, and core; a full lower-body strength builder.
  • Romanian Deadlifts – Focuses on the hamstrings and glutes through a deep hip hinge movement.
  • Walking Lunges – Builds strength, balance, and size in the quads and glutes.
  • Leg Press Machine – Isolates the quads, glutes, and hamstrings with less strain on the lower back.
  • Seated Hamstring Curl Machine – Isolates the hamstrings for knee flexion strength.
  • Standing Calf Raises – Strengthens the calves, focusing mainly on the gastrocnemius muscle.
  • Seated Calf Raises – Targets the soleus muscle of the calves for lower leg thickness.
