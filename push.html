Push Day

Push days focus on exercises that target the chest, shoulders, and triceps. You'll be performing pushing movements to build strength and muscle mass in your upper body.

Sample Push Day Exercises

  • Incline Dumbbell Bench Press – Focuses on the upper chest and front delts.
  • Dips (Lower Chest Focused) – Targets the lower chest and triceps; lean slightly forward to emphasize the chest.
  • Seated Chest Flies or Cable Chest Flies – Isolates the chest with extra focus on the inner chest contraction.
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises or Cable Single-Arm Lateral Raise – Isolates the side delts to build shoulder width.
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – Focuses mainly on the front delts and also works the triceps.
  • Cable Tricep Extension (No Grip Attachment) – Pull the cable directly across the body, keeping the elbow locked in place to isolate the triceps.
  • Overhead Single-Arm Tricep Extension (Pulley Set at Waist Height) – Face away from the pulley, extend straight up; isolates the long head of the triceps.
  • Single-Arm Tricep Extension (Pulley at Highest Position) – Arm positioned slightly behind the body; emphasizes the triceps' full range of motion and stretch.
